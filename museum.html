Grand Egyptian Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum is considered a global cultural icon and the largest museum in the world dedicated to a single civilization, combining the richness of the past with modern architectural design to house the treasures of King Tutankhamun and thousands of rare artifacts.

Situated in a strategic location overlooking the Giza Pyramids, the museum serves as a time gateway connecting visitors to the secrets of ancient Pharaonic civilization through a breathtaking digital and interactive museum experience.
